At the end of my course, students will be able to ...
Create a reasonable set of expectations about what it takes to succeed
Foster a mind set that encourages success
Acquire a skill set that enables success

Who should take this course? Who should not?
You know that you are capable of achieving success in your life, but sometimes experience self-doubt, procrastination and other self-sabotage
Do not take this course if you are looking for some magic trick(s) that will generate success in your life without having to work

What will students need to know or do before starting the course?
You must be able to practice an attitude of gratitude
You must have an open mind
You must be willing to work
